Water Producers-Certified Training Course

This course will provide you with fundamental knowledge and practical skills about how to optimize the operation and maintenance of your water desalination plant. It will help you to reduce your O+M-cost and to understand your responsibility to produce safe drinking water.

  • Theory of Operation of RO-unit
  • Technical equipment and standards
  • Equipment Calibration
  • Monitoring and Controls
  • Routine Maintenance
  • Maintenance and Cleaning Procedures
  • Handling system failures
  • Public Health
  • Water handling
  • Drinking Water Safety
  • Water Quality Testing
  • Emergency preparedness: storage capacity, alternative power supply
  • Business license: how and why?
  • Financial administration
  • Business planning
  • Business growth
  • Business responsibility to provide safe water
